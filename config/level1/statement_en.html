This is a league based challenge.

For this challenge, multiple versions of the same game are available. Once you have proven your skills on this first version, you will access a higher league and extra rules will be unlocked.
This is my first contribution, and it is still work in progress. As such, I haven't implemented leagues.
It means that currently, everything is already available. For future leagues, I plan on making bonuses such as TELEPORT and REVERSE available later. Also, I might only use simple maps (empty, or no teleport) for the starting leagues. Up for discussion.

  The Goal

CodeSnake is a multiplayer game based on the classic single player snake game.
The objective is to reach a size of 12, or to be the last one alive.

  Rules

The game is played on a looping grid (going out of the grid will teleport you on the other side).
Your snake wants to eat Apples, to increase his size, while the opponent snake wants to eat Blueberries.

The Grid is represented by :
- '.' for empty cells.
- '0' for your snake.
- '1' for opponent snake.
- 'A' for the apple.
- 'B' for the blueberry.
- 'W' for a wall.
- 'T' for the TELEPORT Bonus. // Upper league
- 'R' for the REVERSE Bonus. // Upper league

Each turn, you must output a direction (UP, LEFT, RIGHT, DOWN), moving your snake in this direction.
If your head touch a wall or a snake (body or head), you are eliminated.

If your head move on an Apple, the following will occur :
- Your size will increase by 1.
- A new apple will appear at random (but at least 4 cells of any snake head).
You can also eat Blueberries, but it will only make them reappear somewhere else (your length doesn't change).
Note : Everything is reversed for your opponent, so he need to eat Blueberries to increase in size.

In upper leagues, you can also use bonuses (BOOST, TELEPORT).
You'll need to add the bonus you want to use after your move. Example : MOVE REVERSE
REVERSE : Your snake head and tail are swapped ( !! Warning !! Your tail direction is not the same as your head !!)
TELEPORT : Your snake will teleport 2 cells in chosen direction, skipping the next cell. This can be used to jump over walls.

The game last for 200 turns, or until one of the following condition is true :
- 1 player reaches a size of 12
- Only 1 player remains

  Game Input

Input for one game turn
- 1 integer mapSize, the size of the map.
- Next mapSize lines, 1 string row of mapSize char.
- 1 string myDirection, the orientation your snake facing (UP, RIGHT, DOWN, LEFT).
- 1 string myBonuses, a chain of char representing available bonuses (combination of T and R, or NONE if no bonus).
- 1 integer mySize, the size of your snake.
- Next mySize lines, 2 integers x and y representing your snake position.
(Note : Your snake body is in output from head to tail).
- 1 string opponentDirection, the orientation your opponent's snake facing.
- 1 string opponentBonuses, a chain of char representing your opponent's bonuses.
- 1 integer opponentSize, the size of your opponent's snake.
- Next opponentSize lines, 2 integers x and y representing your opponent's snake position.
- the number of valid actions for this turn, validActionCount.
- Next validActionCount lines: 1 string validMove, containing a valid action you can submit.
Output for one game turn
Line 1: 2 space separated string direction and bonus. (Note : you can output just the direction if you don't wish to use a bonus this turn.)
